## Mobile Responsive Considerations

For mobile devices, the layout will adapt as follows:

1. **Navigation**: Convert to hamburger menu
2. **Grid Layouts**: Stack vertically
3. **Sidebars**: Move to bottom or collapse into expandable sections
4. **Tables**: Make scrollable horizontally or reformat for vertical viewing
5. **CTAs**: Ensure buttons are large enough for touch interaction
6. **Images**: Resize appropriately for smaller screens
7. **Text**: Adjust font sizes for readability on mobile devices

## Interactive Elements

The wireframes will include these interactive elements:

1. **Sticky Navigation**: Remains visible while scrolling
2. **Expandable Content Sections**: For FAQs and detailed information
3. **Filtering Options**: For course lists and comparison tables
4. **Hover Effects**: On course cards and buttons
5. **Tooltips**: For additional information on ratings and features
6. **Modal Windows**: For quick course previews
7. **Scroll-to-Top Button**: For long pages
